About this space

Experience unparalleled luxury in our Ocean View Deluxe Suite. Designed for the discerning traveler, this suite harmoniously blends modern aesthetics with coastal tranquility. Spanning 800 sq ft, it features a master bedroom with a plush king-sized bed, a separate spacious living area for entertaining or relaxing, and floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the space with natural light.

Step onto your private balcony to witness breathtaking sunrises over the Atlantic Ocean. The spa-inspired bathroom includes a deep soaking tub, a rain shower, and premium organic toiletries to rejuvenate your senses after a day in the city.
