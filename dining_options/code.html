The Corner Brew

The Corner Brew at Aarya Hotel & Spa is a cozy café and bar located in the heart of Kathmandu's Thamel district. Designed to offer a relaxed atmosphere, it serves a variety of beverages, including specialty coffees, teas, and light snacks. The café's interior blends traditional Nepali elements with modern design, creating a welcoming space for both locals and visitors. Daily Opening Hours: 7 AM to 10 PM.
